Roadmap

Planned design steps once the engine lands —
  • Pile reactions — equilibrium under \(P_u + M_{ux} + M_{uy}\) and check each pile against its allowable load.
  • Cap thickness — iterate \(D_c\) until both punching shear (around the column + around individual piles per ACI 318-14 §13.4.6) and one-way shear pass with adequate margin.
  • Flexure — bending at the critical section (face of column), same SRRB / DRRB classification as Beam & Foundation.
  • Reinforcement schedule — bar count + spacing in both directions, development length, hook geometry per NSCP 2015 §425.
  • Pile anchorage — verify the embedment + bend / hook against the tension demand from any uplift / moment-induced uplift case.
  • Design Schedule — same tabular summary format as the Foundation and Beam calculators so the deliverable is consistent across modules.
